Why We Started

Swift Courier was born from a simple observation: businesses in Kenya needed a delivery service they could trust with their most sensitive items — legal documents, financial records, proprietary samples, and valuable goods.

While general courier services existed, none specialized in the security and chain-of-custody requirements that businesses demand. We built Swift Courier to fill that gap.

Today, we serve over 500 business clients across Nairobi and beyond, from law firms and financial institutions to pharmacies and tech companies. Every delivery is a promise we take seriously.
